Bio

Hi! My name is Nikita.

From the start of my career, I've been hands-on with everything from the ground up: designing prototypes, managing development teams and decomposing upcoming goals. Over the past four years, after transitioning from the corporate world to startups, I've focused exclusively on solving cutting-edge AI problems, while earning my Master's degree and PhD at the same time. This journey has made optimization my life's credo: achieving the most impact with minimal resources. Building things efficiently has, therefore, become a particular strength of mine.

Currently I'm the Co-Founder and CTO at OHMYSYNT with experience from the 500 Global Accelerator.
I share ideas at tech conferences, learn how to grow a business and focus on creating something people truly need.

Since 2021, my responsibilities as CTO included:

  • Executing the product's tech vision and strategy
  • Ensuring team alignment and communication
  • Hiring and managing the development team
  • Optimizing processes and reducing costs
  • Maintaining metrics and unit economics

While obtaining the PhD, I focused on the Generative AI: Text-to-Image and Text-to-Video personalization.
While completing the Master's degree, my field of research included infrared aerial images segmentation, explainable AI in medicine and optimization of the training process of the Vision Transformer.
